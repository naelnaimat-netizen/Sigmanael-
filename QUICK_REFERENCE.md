## When to Use Each Decision Action

### ACT IMMEDIATELY
- Time-sensitive opportunity
- Clear path to success
- Pack is ready
- Resources available
- **Example:** Customer urgent need, you have solution ready

### PREPARE THEN ACT
- High stakes situation
- Complex dependencies
- Need coordination
- Skill gaps exist
- **Example:** Major product launch, market entry

### ACT IF CONVENIENT
- Low priority opportunity
- Spare resources available
- No risk to core mission
- Learning potential
- **Example:** Nice-to-have feature, low-cost experiment

### AVOID
- High risk, low return
- Resources too limited
- Pack not ready
- Better alternatives exist
- **Example:** Unfavorable contract, over-commitment
